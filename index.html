How to read the scores

Admission chance is a screening score derived from the 2025 Phase 2C applicant-to-vacancy ratio. It is not a personal probability.

Overall score combines school quality, admission chance, property investment, family living, transit and value.

Distance evidence is shown in each detail panel. Always retain the cited evidence before making a purchase decision.

Table guide

  • Overall: combined score out of 100.
